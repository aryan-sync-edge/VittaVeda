How We Use and Protect Your Information

Privacy first, security by design.

At VittaVeda, we collect only the information needed to respond to enquiries, provide financial guidance, and improve the service experience across our website and communication channels.

Information you share with us may be used to contact you, prepare consultation responses, and maintain client records. We do not sell your personal data. Any sharing is limited to trusted service providers that help us operate our website or deliver communication, and only where required for service delivery or legal compliance.

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel, and we review our processes to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

What This Statement Covers

Key points at a glance

  • Data collection through forms, calls, and email
  • Limited use of information for consultation and support
  • Basic security controls for website and records
  • Cookie and analytics usage for site improvement
  • Your right to ask for access, correction, or deletion where applicable
